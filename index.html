About Us

Fresh Milk from Our Farms to Your Home

At Arva Dairy, we deliver 100% fresh, pure milk directly sourced from trusted dairy farms and local farmers. Our mission is to provide natural, healthy, and Natural dairy products while supporting sustainable farming practices. Arva Dairy is FSSAI,MSME approved, ensuring that every drop of milk meets the highest safety and quality standards before reaching your home.
